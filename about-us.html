who are we




Square Pride is an industry leader for Online Tiles with 3+ Years of Online Industry Experience. Our Online and Offline store stocks the best range of ceramic,wooden,vitrified tiles and so much more! If you’re looking for satin, matte, polished or textured finishes for indoors or outdoors, look no further. We stock a wide variety of sizes, colours, patterns, textures and finishes ready for delivery to your door step.

Located in Hyderebad, near In Orbit Mall Road, SoftWare Layout, it is here where the company has a physical store present. This Physical Store has enabled Square Pride to become India's first tile company to offer online and offline store of tiles. From beginning to end, only the best will do.Every aspect of our tiles is measured at our very own store, from the tile’s water absorption to its strength. Even the raw materials are constantly tested to ensure that their properties will be worthy of only the best.

We also sell samples of our tiles to help you make the right decision. Simply click on the "Add Sample to cart" button on each product you want sample for.We have great feedback from our customers. Our Suppliers are well known brands including Kajaria,Nitco,Orient Bell,RAK Ceramics & Colortile etc. This includes ranges in floor and wall tiles, mosaics and borders, natural stone claddings, bathroom, kitchen and outdoor. All are available in various sizes, textures and colour, and materials include ceramic ,vitrified, porcelain, natural stone etc.We are now planning to expand to top cities of India Soon.
